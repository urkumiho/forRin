Hey Rin Cubby!

Sweetie Pookie Bear

Dear Rin,

A rose in pink, so shy and sweet,

Like coquette girls with dainty feet.

Her voice—a purr, a soft meow,

Yet gentle as a deer’s low bow.

She tiptoes close, then darts away,

And leaves a bloom where dreams once lay

A kiss in petals, just to say: "Hey."


Ur Sweet Pookie Bear!
